About Pizzeria

Mission Statement

The Pizzeria is your number one source for the best pizza in Toronto . We are an Italian restaurant that specializes strictly in making pizza with various toppings and the freshest ingredients. We strive to serve you with top quality professional customer service. Your happiness and satisfaction is what we work towards. The Pizzeria is the place to go whether you want a quick bite or a party catered. Come visit us!

Our Beginnings

We started as a small local family business and store, beginning with just a stone oven and regular dough and cheese. However, we started to cater for small events, such as birthday parties, meetings, and even weddings. As time passed by, we ended up making enough money to open a new location, and gain more customers in a brand new neighbourhood. Today we have many locations across the GTA and are a vastly known restaurant. We strive too meet our customers needs and wants daily, as without you guys, we would have not been able to make it this far. Thank you, thank you, thank you!!!

Community help

Every year, Pizzeria strive to give back to the community. We sponsor many cultural activities, such as picnics, shows and religious celebrations. Feel free to reach out to us and make a request, we will respond back to you atleast 48 business hours after making you r request. In addition, we feed to homeless twice a year; during the summertime and during the Christmas season. If you would like to help please give us a call or come see us in store and sign up! Your help is truely appreciated.
