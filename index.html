Our Puro Promise

The promise of pure, or as Nonna would say, puro, is the foundation of everything we do. From farmer to your doorstep, you deserve only the best. It’s why we pledge to source only the best ingredients from farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality, and it’s why we use recipes and preparation methods that ensure our food is as fresh as possible. It’s a promise that’s easy to keep - trust us, you’ll taste the difference.

RWA

In 2015, we were the first pizza company to introduce pepperoni made from pork and beef raised without the use of antibiotics.
In 2016, we took it a step further by offering a full slate of proteins free of antibiotics, which include chicken wings, pollini, smoked ham and grilled chicken. We believe in providing our customers the best quality product possible.

Our Business

At Pizzeria, we have been founded upon a culture that is both customer-focused and performance-based. We have a commitment to serving our customers with simply the best quality product. Over the years, we built a team of experienced leaders and a strong operating business model that benefits both our customers and stakeholders.

Our Growth

The story continues to unfold as we strive to meet the demands of our consumers, planning and opening many more restaurants across Ontario and beyond. As we grow, we never lose sight of our mission and unwavering commitment to quality food made from the finest quality ingredients.
